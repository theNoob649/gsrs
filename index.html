Did You Know?

Every solid shape with flat sides follows one neat rule, called Euler's rule:

Corners − Edges + Faces = 2

Count the corners, take away the edges, then add the faces, and you always get 2. A cube gives 8 − 12 + 6 = 2. Try it on any box, prism, or pyramid on this page!
