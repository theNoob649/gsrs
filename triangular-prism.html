Triangular Prism

A triangle tube — like a Toblerone bar

What Is It?

A triangular prism has two matching triangle ends joined by 3 rectangles. Slide a triangle straight through space and the shape it sweeps out is a triangular prism.

Toblerone bars, camping tents and the glass prisms that split light are all this shape.

Properties

You need the triangle's area B, the triangle's perimeter P, and the length L of the prism.

Faces5 — 2 triangles + 3 rectangles
Edges9
Corners (vertices)6
Bases2 matching triangles
Flat or curvedAll flat
Slice it across and you getThe same triangle, all the way along
Euler's rule6 − 9 + 5 = 2 ✓

Formulas

Volume V = B × L

Surface area SA = 2B + P × L

Equilateral base B = (√3⁄4)a²,   P = 3a

Let's Try It!

Take a prism with an equilateral triangle of side a = 4 and length L = 10.

B = (√3⁄4) × 16 = 4√3 ≈ 6.93

V = B × L ≈ 69.28

SA = 2B + 3a × L = 8√3 + 120 ≈ 133.86

Cool Fact

When Isaac Newton shone sunlight through a glass triangular prism, he proved white light is really every colour mixed together.
