Ellipsoid

A stretched or squashed sphere

Description

An ellipsoid is a smooth, closed surface that looks like a sphere stretched or squashed along its axes. It is described by three semi-axes ‚ÄĒ a, b, and c ‚ÄĒ the distances from the center to the surface along each of the three perpendicular directions. When all three are equal (a = b = c) the ellipsoid is simply a sphere.

Real-world ellipsoids include a rugby or American football, an egg (roughly), M&M candies, and ‚ÄĒ on a planetary scale ‚ÄĒ the Earth itself, which bulges slightly at the equator.
