Square

Four equal sides and four square corners

What Is It?

A square is a flat shape with 4 sides that are all exactly the same length, and 4 corners that are all square corners (90°, called right angles).

Chessboard tiles, sticky notes, and the faces of a dice are squares.

Properties

A square needs one number: the side length s.

Sides4 — all equal
Corners4
Each angle90° (a right angle)
All angles add up to360°
Parallel sides2 pairs
Lines of symmetry4
Diagonals2 — equal, and they cross at right angles
Also known asA special rectangle and a special rhombus

Formulas

Area A = s²

Perimeter P = 4s

Diagonal d = s√2

Let's Try It!

Take a square with side s = 6.

A = s² = 36 → 36

P = 4s = 24 → 24

d = s√2 = 6√2 ≈ 8.49

Cool Fact

A square is the answer to a neat puzzle: out of every rectangle with the same perimeter, the square is the one that holds the most area.
