Pentagon

Five sides — like a home plate

What Is It?

A pentagon is a flat shape with 5 straight sides and 5 corners. In a regular pentagon all the sides match and every angle is 108°.

The most famous one is the Pentagon building in America. You'll also find pentagons on a classic football, mixed in with hexagons.

Properties

A regular pentagon needs one number: the side length s.

Sides5 — all equal if regular
Corners5
Each angle108°
All angles add up to540°
Lines of symmetry5
Diagonals5
Tiles a flat floor?No — regular pentagons leave gaps

Formulas

Area A = ¼√(5(5 + 2√5)) × s² ≈ 1.72s²

Perimeter P = 5s

Let's Try It!

Take a regular pentagon with side s = 6.

A ≈ 1.72 × 36 ≈ 61.9

P = 5s = 30

Cool Fact

Draw every diagonal inside a regular pentagon and a five-pointed star appears in the middle.
