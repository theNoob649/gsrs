Rectangle

Four square corners, opposite sides equal

What Is It?

A rectangle is a flat shape with 4 square corners. The two long sides match each other and the two short sides match each other.

Doors, phone screens, books, and this page are all rectangles. A square is just a rectangle where every side happens to be equal.

Properties

A rectangle needs two numbers: the length l and the width w.

Sides4 — opposite sides equal
Corners4
Each angle90°
All angles add up to360°
Parallel sides2 pairs
Lines of symmetry2
Diagonals2 — equal in length, and they cut each other in half

Formulas

Area A = l × w

Perimeter P = 2(l + w)

Diagonal d = √(l² + w²)

Let's Try It!

Take a rectangle with l = 8 and w = 5.

A = l × w = 40 → 40

P = 2(8 + 5) = 26

d = √(64 + 25) = √89 ≈ 9.43

Cool Fact

A4 paper is a clever rectangle: cut it in half and each piece has exactly the same shape as the original, just smaller.
