Trapezoid

Four sides with one pair parallel

What Is It?

A trapezoid is a flat shape with 4 sides where exactly one pair of sides is parallel. Those two parallel sides are called the bases.

Handbags, some bridges, lampshades from the side, and flowerpots from the front are trapezoid shapes.

Properties

You need both bases (a and b) and the height (h) between them.

Sides4
Corners4
Parallel sidesExactly 1 pair (the bases)
All angles add up to360°
Lines of symmetry1 if it is an isosceles trapezoid, otherwise 0
Diagonals2
Basesa (bottom) and b (top)

Formulas

Area A = ½(a + b) × h

Perimeter P = add all four sides

Let's Try It!

Take a trapezoid with bases a = 10 and b = 6, and height h = 4.

A = ½(10 + 6) × 4 = ½ × 16 × 4 = 32

Cool Fact

The area trick is to average the two bases first. A trapezoid holds exactly as much as a rectangle whose width is halfway between its top and bottom.
